Jaret Donahue

I was born in 1997, at a small hospital in a town called Brockton, Massachusetts.
My mom was a stay at home mom who took care of my older three siblings at the time. My dad was a k9 police officer and worked at night mainly. A few years after I was born, I had two more siblings brought into our family. My mom homeschooled all 6 of us through most of elementary school and through all of highschool. When I turned 16 I got my permit and a job shortly following that. My first job was at McDonalds where I worked for 8 months there. Fortunately, I had a friend who worked at a go kart track called F1 Boston . That was an amazing job that I worked until I came to college. College was something I always knew that I needed to do. The problem was I didn't know what I wanted to do. My parents suggested (very heavily) that I go to Bob Jones University . My older three siblings had attended the university and two of them had graduated from it already.I decided to take I.T. while I am down here.
